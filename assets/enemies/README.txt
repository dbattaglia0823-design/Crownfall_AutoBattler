Enemy sprite sheets live here.

Expected format:
- One horizontal sheet per unit.
- 6 equal-width frames.
- Frame order: idle, windup, attack, block, hit, downed.
- Current placeholders are SVG files and can be replaced with your final sheets at the same paths.
